## üõ†Ô∏è Installation Steps
1. **Visit the Releases Page**
   Go to our [Releases page](https://github.com/Affan828/Antibot-Detector-Extension/releases) to find the latest version.

2. **Select the Latest Release**
   Look for the latest version listed. You will see it titled something like "v1.0".

3. **Choose Your Browser**
   You will find download links corresponding to different browsers. Select the link for the browser you use (e.g., Chrome or Firefox).

4. **Download the Extension**
   Click on the download link, which will prompt you to save the file. Follow your browser's instructions to save it to your device.

5. **Install the Extension**
   - For Chrome: 
     - Open Chrome.
     - Go to the top right corner and click on the three dots. Select "More tools" and then "Extensions."
     - Enable "Developer mode" using the toggle in the top right.
     - Click "Load unpacked" and select the folder where you downloaded the extension.
   - For Firefox:
     - Open Firefox.
     - Click on the menu button and select "Add-ons."
     - Click the gear icon and choose "Install Add-on From File." Select the downloaded file.

6. **Enable the Extension**
   After installing, check your browser's extension settings to ensure that the Antibot-Detector-Extension is enabled.

7. **Start Using the Extension**
   Click on the extension icon in your browser. You can now start detecting anti-bot measures on various websites!
